Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view my child's grades?
You can view your child's grades by navigating to the Dashboard section. There you'll find a summary of their current grades and performance in each subject. For detailed assignment scores, click on the specific subject.
How can I contact my child's teacher?
To contact a teacher, go to the Messages section and select the teacher you wish to contact from your contacts list. You can send direct messages or schedule a meeting through the platform.
What should I do if I forgot my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact the school administration.
How do I update my contact information?
To update your contact information, go to Settings > Profile. There you can edit your email address, phone number, and other contact details. Please ensure this information is current so the school can reach you when needed.
How can I set up notifications for my child's activities?
You can customize your notification preferences in the Settings > Notification Settings section. You can choose to receive alerts for new messages, assignment postings, grades, attendance, and school announcements.
